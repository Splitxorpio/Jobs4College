About Us

Why Jobs4College?

Jobs4College was inspired because many college students struggle to find jobs. We want to help fight this problem and make getting a job an easier process. Hence, we decided to make a service that gives college students an option to find jobs that suit their needs; they can choose based on their location, their skills, and many different factors. With our service, these students will have a "head start" on securing a stable job, and will be saved from scrambling to find one once college is over.
